Our Mission

The TechAI Foundation Program is designed to bridge the opportunity gap in technology and data careers. We believe every young person deserves access to quality training, expert mentorship, and real-world experience that opens doors to successful tech careers.

Our comprehensive approach combines hands-on learning with industry connections, ensuring participants don't just learn skills—they build careers.

Connecting Talent to Opportunity

Through our strategic partnerships with Pwani and Nairobi Innovation Weeks, we provide participants with direct access to Kenya's most influential tech ecosystems. These partnerships ensure that our graduates have immediate pathways to internships, employment, and investor connections.

Our network includes leading tech companies, venture capital firms, and innovation hubs across Kenya, creating a robust support system for participant success.
